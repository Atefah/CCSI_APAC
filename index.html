About CCSI-APAC

Compliance and Capacity Skills International - Asia Pacific(CCSI-APAC) was established in Tokyo in 2023 as an Asia-Pacific region affiliate of Compliance and Capacity Skills International (CCSI). CCSI-APAC conducts projects collaborating with the CCSI family-CCSI, CCSI Africa and CCSI UK.

Our Mission

Compliance and Capacity Skills International - Asia Pacific(CCSI-APAC)is a team of experts that supports Governments and industries for international and national security through the non-proliferation of missiles, weapons of mass destruction, and conventional weapons.

Our Project

Our experts with diverse backgrounds and experiences provide cross-cutting,comprehensive advice based on their experience in the United Nations and Governments.

  • We conduct research and seminars on:
  • Security Trade Control, Missile/WMD/Conventional Weapon Non-Proliferation
  • International Sanctions Compliance
  • Cyber Security, Digital Transformation
  • Proliferation Financing
  • Custom and Border Control
  • Maritime and Port Security
  • National Security
  • Economic Statecraft
  • Gender and Human Rights

Based on the unique career, our experts also provide international career development advice.

About the logo and website

Bamboo flourishes in many parts of Asia and the Pacific islands. It is resilient and strong. The CCSI-APAC logo is created with a bamboo motif, symbolizing the growth and prosperity of the region. The logo and website are designed by a team of young Afghanistan designers, trained in the program, Code to Inspire.
